Privacy Policy & Data Access Guide

🛡️ 100% Safe & Local App

This application is built with your absolute privacy in mind. It does not collect, transmit, or monitor any of your data.

💾 Offline Local Storage

All work details (including titles, locations, organizer names, descriptions, and payment details) that you enter are stored only on your own device.

  • Data is stored locally using secure database preferences (SharedPreferences / Web LocalStorage).
  • There is no external server or cloud storage.
  • If you delete or clear the app data, all entered records will be permanently erased. We do not keep any backups.
🚫 Zero Data Access & Permissions

Unlike other apps, Daily Work Tracker does not require any background internet or data collection permissions to operate:

  • No Account Required: You can start tracking your works immediately without providing emails, phone numbers, or passwords.
  • No Internet Permission: The app does not transmit anything online. Your work logs never leave your phone.
  • No Location Access: Even though you can type a location manually, the app does not request GPS or track your position.
🔔 Why Local Permissions are Used

The app requests permissions ONLY for the following core utility features:

  • Notifications: Required to post notification bar alerts when your scheduled "Reminder At" time is reached. You can deny this if you do not want reminders.
  • Vibration / Haptic: Used exclusively to vibrate the phone when the alarm pops up to grab your attention.
💳 Free Trial & Premium Subscription

To support the ongoing development of Daily Work Tracker, the app uses a subscription-based premium model:

  • 1-Week Free Trial: Every new installation includes a 7-day free trial. During this trial period, you can access all features of the app without any restrictions or payments.
  • 1-Year Premium Subscription: To continue using the application after the 7-day trial period expires, a 1-year premium subscription is required.
  • Subscription Terms: Payments are processed securely via Google Play Billing. If you choose not to subscribe after the trial, the app will lock until a subscription is purchased. No payment details are ever collected by the app itself; all transactions are handled entirely by Google Play.
